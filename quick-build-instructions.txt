  BUILD RELEASE APK IN ANDROID STUDIO - QUICK GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

✅ STEP 1: Wait for Gradle Sync
   → Look at bottom right of Android Studio
   → Wait for "Gradle sync finished"
   → Usually takes 1-2 minutes

✅ STEP 2: Build APK
   → Click "Build" in top menu
   → Select "Build Bundle(s) / APK(s)"
   → Click "Build APK(s)"
   → Wait 3-5 minutes

✅ STEP 3: Find APK
   → Click "locate" in notification
   → OR check: android/app/build/outputs/apk/release/app-release.apk

✅ STEP 4: Auto-Copy (Already Running)
   → Script will automatically copy APK when ready
   → Final location: APK_OUTPUT/app-release.apk
